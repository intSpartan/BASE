User authentication:Allow users to sign up, log in, and manage their accounts securely.

Task management:create, edit, and delete tasks.
Assign tasks to specific categories or projects.
Set due dates and priorities for tasks.
Mark tasks as complete.
Search and Filter:implement functionality to search for tasks by keywords and filter tasks based on categories, due dates, priorities, etc.

User dashboard:provide a personalized dashboard where users can view their tasks at a glance, including upcoming tasks, completed tasks, and any overdue tasks.

Notifications:send reminders or notifications to users for upcoming or overdue tasks.

Collaboration:allow users to share tasks or collaborate on projects with other users.

Settings:Include settings where users can customize their preferences, such as notification settings, default views, etc.
